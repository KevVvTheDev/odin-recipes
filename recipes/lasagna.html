Top-tier Lasagna

Description:

This lasagna recipe takes a little work, but it is so satisfying and filling that it's worth it!

  • Spices and seasonings
  • Lasagna noodles
  • Cheese
  • Egg

Steps:

  2. Cook the noodles
  3. Make the ricotta mixture
  4. Assemble the lasagna
  5. Bake the lasagna
